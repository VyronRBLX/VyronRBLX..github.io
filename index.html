Terms of Service

Please read before commissioning work

1. Project Agreement

All projects require a written agreement outlining scope, deliverables, timeline, and payment terms. Changes to scope may result in adjusted pricing and timelines.

2. Payment Terms

  • Projects under $500: Full payment upfront
  • Projects $500-$1000: 50% upfront, 50% on delivery
  • Projects over $1000: 50% upfront, 25% at milestone, 25% on completion
  • All prices in USD unless otherwise specified
  • Refunds considered case-by-case for unstarted work only

The number of revisions included varies by package. Additional revisions are billed at $75/hour. Post-delivery support is provided for the duration specified in your package. Bug fixes for code I wrote are always free within 30 days.

4. Intellectual Property

  • I retain the right to showcase work in my portfolio (unless NDA)
  • Generic libraries/frameworks remain my intellectual property
  • Third-party assets/code will be clearly documented

5. Confidentiality

I respect your privacy and game concepts. All project details are kept confidential. NDAs available upon request for sensitive projects at no additional cost.

6. Delivery & Quality

  • All code is tested before delivery
  • Code follows best practices and is well-documented
  • Source files provided in organized structure
  • Implementation support included

7. Cancellation Policy

Either party may cancel with 48 hours notice. Clients are billed for completed work. Deposits are non-refundable after work has begun.

8. Liability

While I strive for perfection, I cannot guarantee code will be 100% bug-free. I am not liable for any loss of revenue, data, or other damages. My liability is limited to the project cost.
